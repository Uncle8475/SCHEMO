Kotak Junior Scholarship 2023-24



    Eligibility

    • I. Applicants must have secured above 85% marks in Class 10 board examination (SSC/CBSE/ICSE) in 2023.
    • II. Applicant’s annual family income must be Rs. 3,20,000/- or less.
    • III. Applicants must have secured admission in Class 11 at junior colleges/schools in Mumbai for Arts, Commerce and Science Streams, in Mumbai Metropolitan Region* for academic year 2023-24.
    • IV. Children of employees of Kotak Education Foundation and Buddy4Study are not eligible to apply for Kotak Junior Scholarship.

AWARDS

  • Scholarship of INR 3,000 per month 

      • DOCUMENTS

      • I. Income certificate from collector’s office (Either Maharashtra or Indian Govt.) (mandatory)
      • II. Recent passport-size photograph (mandatory)
      • III. PAN card of at least one earning parent/guardian
      • IV. SSC (Class 10) marksheet - e-copy allowed (mandatory)
      • V. School leaving certificate
      • VI. If income tax is paid or an IT return is filed by any family member, then the latest copy of the IT return along with 26AS
      • VII. Student’s bank account (bank statement or first page of bank passbook)

    JM Sethia Merit Scholarship Scheme 2023



      Eligibility

      • I. Be enrolled for a full-time course in one of the following:
      • • Class 9-10 (Category A)
      • • Class 11-12 (Category B)
      • • Graduation course (Category C)
      • • Postgraduation course (Category D)
      • •Professional course (Category P)
      • II. have passed the following examination with the respective aggregate scores: 
      • • Class 8 or equivalent with a 75% aggregate score (Category A)
      • • Class 10 or equivalent with a 75% aggregate score (Category B)
      • • Class 12 or equivalent with a 75% aggregate score in Science & Commerce; 65% in Humanities/Arts (Category C)
      • • Graduation degree with a 60% aggregate in Science & Commerce; 50% aggregate in Humanities/Arts (Category D)
      • • Class 12/Graduation degree with a 60% aggregate in Science & Commerce; 50% aggregate in Humanities/Arts (Category P)

        • AWARD

      • • Category A - INR 400 per month for two years
      • • Category B- INR 500 per month for two years
      • • Category C - INR 600 per month for two or three years as applicable
      • • Category D - INR 700 per month for two or three years as applicable
      • • Category P- INR 1,000 per month for two or three years as applicable

        • DOCUMENTS

      • ◦ Copy of income proof 
      • ◦ Copy of certificate proving reservation (if any reservation is applicable)
      • ◦ Copy of marksheet for the last examination passed

    IDFC FIRST Bank MBA Scholarship 2023-25



      Eligibility

      • •Open for Indian Nationals, residing in India only.
      • • Students enrolled in first year of a 2-year full-time MBA program or its equivalent for the class of 2023 are eligible.
      • • Applicants should be enrolled in any of the MBA colleges mentioned in the attached list.
      • • The gross annual family income of the applicants from all sources should be less than or equal to INR 6 lakh.
      • • Candidates should be no more than 35 years of age on the date of application.
      • • The applicant must have a valid mobile number linked to Aadhaar card.

        • AWARD

      • The selected MBA students will be awarded a scholarship sum of INR 2,00,000 for two years of full-time MBA studies (INR 1 lakh per annum subject to T&C*). 

        • DOCUMENTS

      • • Admission related documents
      • ◦ Admission Proof (With name of institution, admission year and batch) 
      • ◦ Copy of Fee Receipt 
      • • Copy of Graduation marksheet/passing certificate
      • • Copy of marksheet for the last examination passed
      • • Income Proof (any one)
      • ◦ Income Certificate issued by relevant government authority (Tehsildar or Magistrate)
      • ◦ Admission Proof (With name of institution, admission year and batch) 
      • ◦ Copy of Salary Slip 
      • • Other Documents (If applicable)
      • ◦ Disability certificate
      • ◦ Sanctioned loan letter
      • ◦ Death certificate, if there is a death of the sole earning member of the immediate family
